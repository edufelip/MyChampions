# Product Vision V1

## Vision
A subscription wellness app where students connect with nutritionists and fitness coaches to receive personalized meal and training plans, while still supporting self-managed progress when no professional is assigned.

## Core Value
- Students get clear daily guidance for food and training.
- Professionals manage many students from one mobile dashboard.
- Plan creation is template-based but highly customizable per student.
- Professionals can maintain named predefined plan libraries and use bulk assignment with per-student fine-tuning.
- Users can create reusable custom meals and log consumed grams with automatic calorie/macro calculation.
- Users can share custom recipes by link so others can save independent copies to their own accounts.
- Saved shared copies stay in recipient accounts even if original creators delete their source recipes.
- Hydration support includes daily water tracking with streak visibility and configurable goals.

## Persona Overview
- Student:
  - Wants structured progress with simple daily tracking.
  - Can operate with professional guidance or self-managed mode.
- Nutritionist:
  - Creates meal templates and customizes by calories/macros.
  - Monitors student adherence.
- Fitness Coach / Personal Trainer:
  - Creates training templates and customizes sessions.
  - Monitors execution and progression.

## Critical Business Constraint
A student can have only:
- One active nutritionist.
- One active fitness coach.

The same professional can fill both specialties if dual-qualified.

## Product Pillars
- Role-based journeying (Student vs Professional).
- Assignment governance and relationship lifecycle.
- Nutrition planning with calorie/macro structure and food API support.
- Custom meal library with proportional-by-grams nutrition logging.
- Recipe sharing with copy-on-save ownership.
- Training planning with reusable and customizable session schemas.
- Daily student tracking and adherence feedback.
- Hydration tracking with student-defined and nutritionist-defined water goals.

## MVP Intent (Draft)
- Deliver end-to-end role onboarding.
- Deliver assignment model with one-per-specialty constraints.
- Deliver plan authoring and assignment for both specialties.
- Deliver student tracking for daily nutrition and workouts.
